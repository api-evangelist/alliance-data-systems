Shaping our approach to AI, agent-driven commerce Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping how customers discover, evaluate and purchase products, with this shift toward agent-driven commerce unfolding in stages. Today, AI supports discovery while consumers make final decisions and complete transactions. Over time, this is expected to evolve toward more integrated and near-real-time checkout experiences, with increasing levels of automation.
